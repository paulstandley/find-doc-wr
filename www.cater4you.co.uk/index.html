Cater For You Ltd

Cater For You Limited was established in 2003 as an on-line wholesaler of plastic glasses and food packaging.  Our basic business principle is to where possible hold all of our product lines in stock and be able to ship all orders placed by 2pm for next working day delivery (Monday to Friday standard delivery option). We have always shipped across the whole of the UK but now also have extensive European shipping options on-line.  All of this is undertaken from our 17,000sq ft warehouse in High Wycombe, Buckinghamshire.

We have a huge range of plastic glasses for every occasion from multi pallet events to your home bar-b-q.  There is a broad choice of style including plastic wine glasses, plastic champagne glasses and plastic pint glasses in either virtually unbreakable, reusable or disposable.

Food Packaging has many roles for our customers, keeping the food safe for consumption, suitable protection for retail display, take aways and increasingly for delivery direct to the customer.  We have a wide range of sections with many products including take away food packaging suitable for food protection and also premium retail display. 

A product range we introduced in 2008 and is consistently one of our most popular is the Baking and Cake Decorating section, ideal for home and professional bakers alike. We sell a wide choice of cake boxes in particular as well as cupcake cases and decorating equipment.

We’ve always been pro-active at encouraging recycling of our products and were one of the first to research and feature which products could be recycled with the relevant recycling code. We also have a dedicated section of biodegradable food packaging and catering disposables.

On each product page we have more information on the product, often several photos and bulk pricing for our larger customers.  To give you independent information and peace of mind, we also pay for FEEFO and independent review system which gives you both service and product information.

We like to think our web site is simple to use for anyone, but if you have any queries about the products, lead times, delivery etc then please contact one of the team by email or by phone and we will be more than happy to help. If you’d like to try before you buy, then please request a sample.
